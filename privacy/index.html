Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

PitlaneStats (pitlanestats.com) is a static website dedicated to Formula 1 statistics. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we do — and don't — collect when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

PitlaneStats is a static website. We do not operate user accounts, login systems, or databases that store visitor information. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our visitors.

Specifically, we do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, location data, or any other personally identifiable information through the site itself.

Cookies

PitlaneStats does not use cookies. We do not set first-party cookies, and we do not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand general traffic patterns such as page views and referral sources. Any analytics data collected is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot be used to identify individual visitors.

Third-Party Services

Our site loads resources from the following third-party services for functionality and performance:

Google Fonts — for web typography (Space Grotesk and Inter). Google may collect limited technical data when serving fonts. See Google's privacy policy for details.
Cloudflare CDN — for delivering CSS and JavaScript libraries. Cloudflare may process limited technical data for security and performance purposes.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties.

External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

Our site is a general-audience statistics reference. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any visitor, no special provisions are required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

info@pitlanestats.com
