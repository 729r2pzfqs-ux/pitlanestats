About the 2026 F1 Calendar

The 2026 Formula 1 World Championship runs across 22 Grand Prix weekends, opening with the Australian Grand Prix in Melbourne on 8 March and closing with the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix at Yas Marina on 6 December. The season introduces all-new technical regulations and sustainable fuels.

Six events host F1 Sprint races in 2026: China, Miami, Canada, Great Britain, the Netherlands and Singapore. Madrid joins the calendar for the first time, hosting the Spanish Grand Prix at the new Madring street circuit.
